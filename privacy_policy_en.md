# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 22, 2026**  
**Effective Date: February 22, 2026 (v1.0.0)**

> **[Key Highlights]**
> *   **Items Collected:** Email (for account), Birth year (for generation-based services), Location (optional), Photos.
> *   **Retention Period:** Until account deletion (or as required by law).
> *   **Third-party Provision:** We use Google Firebase (cloud infrastructure) for smooth service operation.

'SkyStory' (hereinafter referred to as the 'App') establishes and discloses this Privacy Policy in accordance with Article 30 of the Personal Information Protection Act to protect the personal information of users and to process related grievances promptly and smoothly.

---

### 1. Purpose of Processing Personal Information
The App processes personal information for the following purposes. We will not use personal information for purposes other than those specified below, and if the purpose changes, we will implement necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent.

1. **Membership Registration and Management:** Confirming the intention to join, identifying and authenticating individuals, maintaining and managing membership, and preventing unauthorized use of services.
2. **Service Provision:** Location-based memory recording (optional), photo uploading and management, community features (likes, comments), and operating generation-based ranking systems.

### 2. Items of Personal Information Processed
The App collects the minimum amount of personal information necessary to provide services.

| Category | Items Collected | Purpose of Collection and Use |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Required** | **Email address**, **Birth year** | Account identification, password reset, generation-based ranking, and customized services |
| **Optional** | **Location info (GPS)** | Recording the city name when creating a post |
| **Automatic** | Service use records, access logs, cookies, device info, ad identifiers | Service improvement, prevention of unauthorized use, statistical analysis |

### 3. Processing and Retention Period of Personal Information
1. The App processes and retains personal information within the period specified by law or the period consented to by the user at the time of collection.
2. The personal information retention periods are as follows:
   - **Membership Registration and Management:** **Until account deletion**. However, if separate preservation is required by law, until the period specified by the relevant law.

### 4. Provision and Consignment to Third Parties
The App entrusts or provides personal information as follows for smooth service provision:

| Recipient / Trustee | Category | Purpose | Items Provided | Retention Period |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Google Cloud (Firebase)** | Provision / Consignment | Cloud infrastructure (Auth, DB, Storage, Push notifications) | Email, birth year, location, photo data | Until account deletion or service termination |

### 5. Rights and Obligations of Users and Their Exercise Methods
As a data subject, users can exercise their rights such as viewing, correcting, deleting, and requesting suspension of personal information processing at any time. Rights can be exercised through the App settings or via email, and the App will take action without delay.

### 6. Destruction Procedures and Methods
1. **Procedure:** The App destroys personal information without delay when it becomes unnecessary, such as when the retention period has expired or the purpose has been achieved.
2. **Method:** Information in electronic file format is deleted using technical methods that cannot reproduce the record.

### 7. Measures to Ensure the Safety of Personal Information
The App is taking the following measures to ensure the safety of personal information:
1. **Administrative:** Internal management plans, regular staff training, etc.
2. **Technical:** Access rights management, installation of access control systems, encryption of unique identification information (applying Firebase security protocols).
